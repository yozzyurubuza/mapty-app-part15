Final Considerations

10 Additional Feature Ideas: Challenges

Easy ones:
1. Ability to edit a workout
2. Ability to delete a workout;
3. Ability to delete all workouts;
4. Ability to sort workouts by a certain field (e.g distance)
5. Re-build Running and Cycling objects coming from Local Storage (because the objects coming from the localStorage are transformed into regular objects, no more inheritance).
6. More realistic error and confirmation messages

Hard ones:
7. Ability to position the map to show all workouts (especially when workout position are very far from each other).
8. Ability to draw lines and shapes instead of just points.

After the next section: [Only after asynchronous JavaScript section]
9. Geocode location from coordinates ("Run in Faro, Portugal").
10. Display weather data for workout time and place.
